Welcome

The AgriExpense mobile application was developed to assist farmers to keep records of the operational cost of each crop cycle, by recording the crop expenses on the go and monitoring cost of production per unit harvested.

Cycles

Cycles represent the individual sowing of a crop within a unit of land. Farmers may have multiple cycles of the same crop, made ad crop-specific intervals to ensure consistent flow of harvests.

Purchases

Purchases represent the total cost of the item purchased. As materials from the purchased are used during the life cycle of the crop, the fraction of the cost will be added to the cycle.
